Who is Dojo?

Background

Dojo is a Vosloo-based Hip Hop Group. We focus primarily on Boom-Bap, but we are not limited to one particular sound. We embody the elements of Hip Hop with our lyricism and a sound that keeps maturing as we grow. Come and join us on our journey and be a part of our progressive Hip Hop community.
